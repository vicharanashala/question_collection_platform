# Privacy Policy

**Annam.Ai — AnnaDatha Platform**

Effective Date: 30 June 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

**Annam.Ai** ("we," "us," or "our") operates the AnnaDatha mobile application (the "Platform"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect information about you when you use the AnnaDatha Platform. By using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you register and use the Platform, you may provide us with:

- **Mobile number** — used for account registration and OTP verification
- **Name** — used to identify your account
- **Category** — farmer, trader, input dealer, or other stakeholder classification
- **Location data** — state, district, block, and village (used to tailor content and connect you with relevant community members)
- **KVK (Krishi Vigyan Kendra)** affiliation, if applicable
- **Language preference** — to deliver content in your preferred language
- **Profile photo** — optional, uploaded by you
- **Questions, answers, and comments** — content you submit to the community
- **Chat messages** — direct messages you send to other users

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access the Platform, we may automatically collect:

- **Device information** — device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
- **App usage data** — pages viewed, features used, and interaction patterns
- **IP address** — used for session management and basic analytics
- **Login timestamps** — for security and audit purposes

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from government LGD (Local Government Directory) APIs when you select your block and village, to validate and store your geographic location accurately.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account
- Authenticate your identity via OTP
- Personalize your experience based on your location and preferences
- Deliver questions, answers, and community content relevant to you
- Enable direct messaging between users
- Maintain platform security and prevent unauthorized access
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or illegal activity
- Send you service-related notifications (e.g., OTP, account alerts)
- Respond to your support requests and queries
- Improve, test, and monitor the performance of the Platform
- Use your voice and audio recordings (where you choose to submit them) to train, improve, and evaluate our AI models and speech recognition systems
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 Service Providers
We share data with trusted third-party vendors who help us operate the Platform — including cloud hosting providers, SMS gateway services (for OTP delivery), and analytics tools. These providers are contractually bound to use your data only to provide services to us.

### 4.2 Community Features
Content you post publicly — including your questions, answers, and profile name — may be visible to other users of the Platform. Your mobile number is not displayed publicly.

### 4.3 Direct Messages
Messages you send to other users are visible only to you and the recipient.

### 4.4 Legal Compliance
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of Annam.Ai, our users, or the public.

### 4.5 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via the Platform before your data is transferred and subject to a different privacy policy.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Retention

- Your personal data is stored on secure servers operated by Annam.Ai or our cloud service providers.
- **Account data** is retained for as long as your account is active, and for a period of up to 3 years after account deletion or deactivation.
- **Audit logs** (login timestamps, security events) are retained for a minimum of 1 year for security and compliance purposes.
- **Content you post** (questions, answers) may be retained even after account deletion, in anonymized or aggregate form, to maintain platform integrity.

When we no longer need your data, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration, including:

- Encrypted transmission (HTTPS) for all data in transit
- Password hashing (bcrypt) for stored credentials
- Role-based access controls within our systems
- Regular security reviews and monitoring

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
